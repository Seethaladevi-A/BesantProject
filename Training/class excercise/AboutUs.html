Expense tracking is the process of recording and analyzing your expenses to understand your budget.
It can be done for a specific project or over a period of time.
You can use an expense tracker to track your personal expenses, business expenses, or both.

How to use :

  • Record all your expenditures, including one-time and variable costs
  • Track your spending over a specific period, such as a week or month
  • Review your spending to identify areas where you can cut back or save more
